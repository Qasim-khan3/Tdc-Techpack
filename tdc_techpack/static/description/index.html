What is TDC Techpack?

A Tech Pack is the master specification document that fashion brands send to manufacturers before production. It defines exactly how a garment must be made — fabric, fit, stitching, hardware, labels, and costing. Without a clear Tech Pack, factories guess. Guessing leads to mistakes, delays, and wasted money.

TDC Techpack is a complete Odoo 18 module that lets your team create, approve, and print professional specification sheets — all inside Odoo, connected directly to your Sales and Manufacturing workflows.

Sale & Manufacturing Integration
🛒 Sale Order Integration

A Tech Pack smart button appears on every Sale Order form.

Sales staff can view or create linked Tech Packs without leaving the Sale Order.

Link multiple Sale Orders to one Tech Pack, or one Sale Order to multiple Tech Packs.

⚙️ Manufacturing Order Integration

A Tech Pack smart button appears on every MRP Production form.

Production teams access the exact specification they need without searching.

Reduces errors caused by outdated or missing spec documents on the factory floor.
